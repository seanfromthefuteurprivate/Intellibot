## ANTI-PATTERNS: WHAT TO AVOID

### ANTI-PATTERN 1: The Pre-10 AM Entry

**Why it fails:** Retail is still active, algos are running stop hunts, true direction not established.

**Example:**
```
9:35 AM: "Perfect" Gap and Crap setup, all signals present
9:40 AM: Enter puts at $245
9:42 AM: Surprise spike to $248 on headline (stop hit, -$800)
10:05 AM: Real fade begins to $238 (would have been +$2,400)
```

**Lesson:** Wait for 10 AM. ALWAYS. No exceptions.

---

### ANTI-PATTERN 2: Fading Strong Catalysts

**Why it fails:** Earnings beats, M&A announcements, FDA approvals create sustained momentum.

**Example:**
```
Company reports 40% earnings beat + raises guidance
Gap +8%, you see "Gap and Crap" pattern
Enter fade at 10:05 AM
Price continues to $15% by 2 PM (stop hit, -$1,200)
```

**Lesson:** Respect catalyst quality. Soft news fades. Hard news continues.

---

### ANTI-PATTERN 3: The Lunch Trade

**Why it fails:** No volume, no conviction, random chop.

**Example:**
```
12:15 PM: "Perfect" VWAP rejection
12:20 PM: Enter puts
12:45 PM: Price bounces back (no volume to sustain move)
1:30 PM: Exit -$400
2:15 PM: Real move happens when volume returns (missed it)
```

**Lesson:** 11:30 AM - 2:00 PM is dead zone. Stay out.

---

### ANTI-PATTERN 4: Chasing After Missing Entry

**Why it fails:** You missed optimal entry, now you're emotionally trading.

**Example:**
```
10:05 AM: Perfect entry at $241 (you hesitated)
10:15 AM: Price at $238, "still going down" (FOMO)
10:18 AM: Enter puts at $238
10:25 AM: Dead cat bounce to $241 (stop hit, -$600)
10:50 AM: Price continues to $235 (would have worked but you got shaken)
```

**Lesson:** If you miss the entry window, wait for next setup. Don't chase.

---

### ANTI-PATTERN 5: Ignoring Volume Divergence

**Why it fails:** Price direction without volume confirmation is noise.

**Example:**
```
Gap and Crap pattern present
VWAP broken
All signals except... volume is INCREASING on move higher
You ignore volume, enter fade
Result: Gap and Go disguised as Gap and Crap, -$900
```

**Lesson:** Volume is truth. Price is story. Never trade the story without the truth.
